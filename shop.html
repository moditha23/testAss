To qualify for the special, legal distinction denoted by the words 'Ceylon Tea,' as well as the famous Lion logo that goes with it, the tea must not only be grown and manufactured entirely in Sri Lanka, but it must also adhere to strict quality standards established and enforced by the Sri Lanka Tea Board. Furthermore, it cannot be mixed or blended with tea from any other part of the world. Even a 95% Sri Lankan blend cannot be described as Ceylon Tea.
